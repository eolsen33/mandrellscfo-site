01 What we handle

Eight disciplines, one accountable team — the full operational accounting stack, short of filing your taxes.

01
Fractional CFO

Custom budgets, forecasts, and financial roadmaps. Department-level efficiency analysis, contract and banking negotiation, and control reviews that catch leaks before they cost you.

02
Bookkeeping

Multiple service levels — from monthly close to full back-office. Bank and credit-card reconciliations, AR, AP, general ledger, and financial statements you can read.

03
Payroll Processing

Timely, accurate pay, multi-state where required. Tax deposits, payroll tax returns, and specialized handling of garnishments — your team gets paid; you get the records.

04
Human Resources

Recruiting, onboarding, regulatory compliance, benefits administration, and organizational culture. We advise leadership on performance management and staff development.

05
Internal Audits & Controls

Periodic internal reviews of policies, procedures, and systems against industry best practices — with the control changes that mitigate loss and reduce exposure.

06
Sales & Payroll Tax

Sales, use, and payroll tax returns — monthly, quarterly, annual, or semi-weekly. Florida and multi-jurisdiction. Submitted accurately, on time, and audit-ready.

07
Cash Management & Forecasting

Budget creation, financial monitoring, collection-cycle analysis, and vendor payment timing — so cash works for you, not against you.

08
Staff Training & Development

Comprehensive training in accounting and HR for your in-house team. Internal advancement, not dependence — we leave you stronger than we found you.

At year-end, we package everything your CPA needs to file. No scramble in February, no missing receipts, no awkward emails — just a clean binder.

04 The Firm

A family that does the books for families that built businesses.

Mandrell's CFO Services was founded in 2018 by Christina and Shane Mandrell — a husband-and-wife team with a combined four decades of corporate finance and business ownership.

Christina spent twenty-one years inside the books of growing companies — including Inc. 5000 firms — as a corporate controller across automotive, e-commerce, and operations. She runs the firm the way she once ran a controllership: with discipline, calm, and a refusal to be surprised in February.

Shane is a retired Navy reservist, a University of Florida engineer, and the owner-operator of Mandrell's Pressure Cleaning — five locations from North Central Florida to Tampa. He brings the operator's view to every engagement.

Together, they built a firm that runs on something simple: match the right people to the right business — and stay close enough to actually know it.
