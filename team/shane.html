Shane
Mandrell.

Shane Mandrell is a retired Navy Reserve Vet with 20 years of military service and has over 25+ years business ownership experience, since 1999. He is part of the Gator Nation through and through, graduating from the University of Florida in 2004 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering along with a double minor in Business Management and Sales Engineering. He partnered with his wife in 2018, to begin their company to service other businesses with operational accounting needs.

Shane's business expertise comes from his other business, Mandrell's Pressure Cleaning, LLC. with 5 offices stretching across North Central Florida down to Tampa. He knows firsthand the value and impact of advertising, marketing, social media and the importance of sound financial management. His expertise includes Microsoft Database technology & programming, local wired/wireless networking topologies, numerous computer platforms, programming languages & office suites, WordPress, SEO Optimization, AdWords and launching a business from the ground up.

In his spare time, Shane enjoys travel, and watching live sports — especially football, the Tampa Bay Lightning and any Gator sport here in the Swamp or around the University of Florida campus; Go Gators!
