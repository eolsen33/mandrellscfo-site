Christina
Mandrell.

Christina Mandrell has over 21 years managerial experience in the Corporate Accounting field since 2005. She has practiced as a Corporate Controller for several large groups, in the automotive and e-commerce industries based out of Northeast and Central Florida. Graduating from Southeastern University with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting and a minor in Business Management in 2005; Christina Mandrell partnered with her husband to begin their company to service other businesses in 2018.

Christina specializes in providing expert accounting and business management services for a variety of companies. Over the course of her career she has developed an excellent reputation for her expertise in various issues of corporate compliance, strategic direction of cash management and business operations from a financial perspective. From corporate headquarters to a small online startup that has made the national Inc. 5000 list of fastest growing companies in America several years running, Christina has provided invaluable strategic leadership through some of the toughest economic times this generation of entrepreneurs has ever faced.

In her spare time, Christina loves to travel and watch live sports — especially the Tampa Bay Lightning and any Gator sport here in the Swamp or around University of Florida campus.
